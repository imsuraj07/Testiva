Who We Are

Testiva is a professional online test portal designed to conduct secure, scalable, and accurate digital examinations. Our system supports academic tests, competitive exams, recruitment assessments, and institutional evaluations.

With real-time result generation, automated evaluation, and advanced analytics, Testiva helps both students and educators make better academic decisions.
